Why Physical Fitness Matters


A Sedentary Lifestyle And Elevated Stress Levels Are Not Uncommon In Corporate Executives. Insufficient Physical Activity Leads To A Decline In Energy, Stamina And Health, Which Adversely Affects Productivity And Performance. Aside From Reducing The Risk Of Heart Disease, High Blood Pressure, High Cholesterol, Stroke, Type 2 Diabetes And Certain Types Of Cancer, Regular Exercise Keeps You Energised And Helps You Carry Out Your Daily Activities More Efficiently.

RMTD GYM INTERNATIONAL


RMTD gym has been the authority in fitness since 1965 dating back to the original RMTD gym in venice, california. It was the place for serious fitness. Opened long before the modern day health club existed, the original RMTD gym featured homemade equipment and a dedication to getting results. It was an instant hit. RMTD gym quickly became known as “the mecca of bodybuilding.” In 1977, RMTD gym received international attention when it was featured in the movie pumping iron that starred arnold schwarzenegger and lou ferrigno. From that first gym in venice, RMTD gym has become the largest co-ed gym chain in the world with over 701+ facilities in 30 countries. Today, RMTD gym has expanded its fitness profile to offer wellness programs which includes all of the latest equipment and services, including group exercise, personal training, cardiovascular equipment, spinning, and yoga, while maintaining its core weight lifting tradition. RMTD gym has become the preferred health and wellness gym of celebrities, athletes, bodybuilders, the military and fitness enthusiasts all over the world. Most importantly, RMTD gym continues to change lives by helping people achieve their individual potential..
